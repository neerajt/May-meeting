Polygene Clusters

Approach

  • Used log-odds ratios from PGC2 paper to create polygene risk scores

  • Optimal cutoff for the PGC2 list was computed using Nagelkerke r square

  • Original sample used Genomas SNP data
    • 164 SZ, 112, SZA, 205 PBP (118 BT1, 133 BT2, 187 BT3)
  • Validation sample used PsychChip SNP data
    • 132 SZ, 53 SZA, 19 PBP (68 BT1, 73 BT2, 63 BT3)

  • Both datasets subjected to the same QC procedures
